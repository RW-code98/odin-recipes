Lemon Zucchini Bread

Description

Zucchini bread is a slightly sweet quick bread (similar to a muffin), with a hint of cinnamon. It's often punctuated with tender bits of walnut, like you see here in this recipe. The zucchini itself is so mild, you don't even really taste it at all.

Ingredients

  • 1 ½ cups shredded zucchini
  • ¾ cup white sugar
  • 1 egg
  • ½ cup vegetable oil
  • 1 ½ cups all-purpose flour
  • ½ teaspoon salt
  • ½ teaspoon baking soda
  • ¼ teaspoon baking powder
  • 1 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • 2 teaspoons lemon zest
